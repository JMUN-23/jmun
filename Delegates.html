Delegate Registration

Note:

1)The conference shall take place in school premises from 27th - 29th July, 2023 .

2)There shall be a Mock Training session for delegates on 26th of July 2023.

3)Accomodation charges for outstation teams are excluded from the registeration fees. Payment options and accomodation charges will be shared once the participation in the conference is confirmed by the school.

4)Once the Registeration form has been submitted, the delegation will be contacted by the Secreteriat and will be asked to make the payment

5)For the Global Press Summit, the applicants will be asked to make the payment only after they are informed about their application being accepted

6)Minimum size of delegation is 8 delegates. The delegates may be from classes 7th to 12th

7)The Registeration fee for each delegate is 2500 Rs. and 1000 Rs. for each faculty advisor. This covers High tea and lunch on all 3 days along with stationery
