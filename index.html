Letter from the Secretary General

Greetings,

Greetings, I am highly ecstatic to invite you to the twelfth edition of the Jaipuria Model United Nations, from 27-29 july,2023. This edition of JMUN guarantees you the best of enthrallment and intellect simultaneously. This is not just a conference for me but a festival, a festival that has a special place in my heart which cannot be ever replaced by anything and thus I will make sure it is the best experience that you ever have. It is a MUN conference for other people but to our Secretariat it is an emotion as strong as of love. The first MUN conference in Uttar Pradesh will always be one of its kinds and cannot be imitated. The legacy of JMUN has been ever since continued when it started in 2013. This July we present to you eight very interesting and involving committees from the un-guessable G20 to the highly awaited by everyone, Lok Sabha. The tradition of the Global Press Summit the only one of its kind in JMUN will be yet continued again. As we are getting closer to this grand festival in July which surely going to be the best conference you will ever encounter, I Shlok Kapoor the Secretary General of Jaipuria Model United Nations would like to welcome you all to our twelfth edition.

Regards,
Secretary General.
