G20 Summit

AGENDA- “Deliberating upon the Obstructive Strategies Employed by the Anti-Green Energy Groups and their Effect upon the Green Energy Revolution"

With the changing paradigms of the globe, the green energy revolution is where our hope lies. With the gleam of solar panels, the whirl of the turbines, the swirl of the windmills and the unrelenting force of the oceanic currents, the onus to build a robust and sustainable future lies on us. As countries come together to reduce fossil fuel consumption and make a shift towards greener options, ‘anti-green energy groups’ haunt the green elysium with their selfish business driven pursuits. Restoring the sempiternal radiance and the majestic charm of the planet and its captivating and pristine fashion, we long for a world of abundance, ataraxy and balance. The Delegates must decide as to how Sustainable future can be reached as it is the need of the hour.

Global Press Summit (GPS)

AGENDA- “Media Ownership Affecting Media Plurality.”

The sacred edifice of democracy is uprightly established upon its four esteemed pillars. Media- the fourth pillar, is the voice of the masses, interpreting and commenting on the present and setting the agenda for the future. Plurality, in the media context, refers to the availability of fair, balanced and unbiased representation of a wide range of opinions and views. If the same media entity controls outlets across different media segments, it raises the concern that similar views and opinions are being disseminated across all segments, thereby limiting plurality. As we take forward the legacy of the committee exclusive to the Jaipuria Model United Nations, we invite you to safeguard the fourth pillar of democracy from keeling over.

The Joint Crisis Committee

AGENDA- The Sino-Indian War, 1962.

"Love thy neighbour as thyself."
It is the 18th of October, 1962. Chilly air, as cold as the hearts throbbing in the valiant chests of the comrades, descends on the majestic mountains of Aksai Chin. As the echoes of the flouncing steps of the combatants fill the air, the tension between the stern neighbours seems to soar as high as the lofty peaks. Not far away, the frontier camps are witnessing an uproar, tanks are being fuelled with anathema, and ruses are diplomatically being engendered. Troops on either side are advancing. The vast horizon of the future remains obscure as the resolute minds are taken back to their purpose. But peace was a fleeting friend. And, FREEZE. Now, history waits in the wings to be refashioned on our lines as we invite delegates to Deliberate, Defend and Deliver upon the border dispute that ignited the flame in the Himalayan range.

Jobs Reset Summit (JRS)

AGENDA- “Deliberating upon the future of Gig and Platform economy and its aspects.”

Like the sapphire blue waves of an ocean that rise and fall unpredictably, business cycles are unequivocally erratic. Economies worldwide have witnessed booms, depressions, progress and recessions as an impending result of the pandemic, the precariousness of layoffs looming around the working population and inflation hitting its zenith. As the waves ascend and subside, a new economic model emerges to the surface- Gig Economy. It provides an opportunity to the human race who yearn to work unconstrained and unbridled. The multitude of capacities dispensed by it in the online and offline sectors of various professions is unparalleled. The question that arises is whether the Gig and Platform economy, the future of the world or just a mere delusion?

Lok Sabha (LS)

AGENDA- 1) “Discussing the withdrawal of the Personal Data Protection Bill, 2019 and deliberating upon the new draft of the Digital Data Protection Bill,2022.”
2) “Discussing the proposal for same marriage and civil laws with special reference to The Prohibition of Child Marriage (Amendment) Bill,2021 and its overriding effect over other marriage laws”

The digital age is celebrated as a vision to digitally transform India. However, there is a flip side to the coin that most are oblivious to. A gamut of citizens' data is assured to be safe, but is it? Should the government have access to citizens' data? With the recent withdrawal of the Personal Data Protection Bill 2019, the need for a well-defined, comprehensive document regarding this issue has become evermore relevant.
In the esteemed library of the Parliament lies the quintessence of glory, a certain book that holds great reverence. Words etched on a page declare our country secular. Still, India has marriage and civil laws, which differ with different religions and regions. Thus, it is necessary to deliberate on the issue of necessity for uniform marriage laws along with the disparity in the marriageable age for among genders. We leave it on you, Parliamentarians, to take up some crucial issues in the spirit of Indian Democracy. “यह देश रहना चाहिए। यह लोकतंत्र अमर रहना चाहिएI”

The United Nations General Assembly (DISEC)

AGENDA- "Deliberating upon the Ethics of Modern Warfare with special emphasis on the Third Revolution in Warfare."

As we step into the Third Revolution of Warfare, our minds are swamped with dubiety over what artificial intelligence may bring forth. Every passing day, as machines overtake human rationale with impressive and unprecedented potential, the notion of warfare ethics fades into oblivion. The proliferation of artificial intelligence in warfare, the ascendancy of cyber warfare and the ubiquitous use of unmanned vehicles, including drones, have all obfuscated the boundaries between combatants and non-combatants, and perhaps between moral and immoral. Time, therefore, calls upon us to address the dawn or doom this new age of warfare brings upon humanity. As we usher into a new era of Jaipuria Model United Nations, we hope that the delegates will ponder upon the pressing need of ethics in modern warfare as it augments and the cry of exigency calls out to them.

United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC)

AGENDA- “Addressing the increased exploitation of Human Resources in the Mining Sector”

Where the beam of light radiating from their headbands is the only light they see in the vast expanse of gloom, that reminds them of the subdued ray of light in their hearts that dares to hope. The bleeding wings of freedom of these chained prisoners are slashed and wounded by self-seeking human ambitions. Most scriptures expound on Heaven and Hell as places where Justice is served with righteousness-either eternal reward or punishment. This hell has left the innocent bereft of their basic human rights, with violations transpiring in forms of child labour, forced labour and malignant working conditions which may leave an everlasting detrimental scar on their health. The onus to safeguard the flickering light of hope lies on the Delegates as they come together to Deliberate, Defend and Deliver on the violation of basic human rights.

United Nations Security Council (UNSC)

AGENDA - “The Syrian Crisis, 2026"

A land in the Mediterranean has turned to a hopeless shade of sienna, burnt like the dreams of normal life and reduced it to ashes. Though the sky above is clear and blue, the menacing black dense clouds of an uncertain future and an ever worsening present seem to hover persistently. Bombs roar like a hungry beast; the staccato beat of the guns’ fire, the lethal thunder of bullets, a trail of despoliation all around, a chorus of screams and a fervent hanker of sobriety. The human tragedy in Syria is a lugubrious reality and evidence of a shared vulnerability. Ever since the outbreak of the civil war, a land devastated by the war demon has been looked upon by nations as a land of opportunities for their own selfish motives, turning it into a geopolitical battlefield. As we summon you to the futuristic United Nations Security Council, we implore the delegates to rack your brains and ideate solutions to not merely resolve the Syrian crisis but catastrophes which gripple the entire International community, perhaps another cold war scenario, an inevitable war at sea or the apparently ended Russo-Ukrainian War. Amidst the most excruciating crises and gravest of mayhem, do you possess the power to foresee?
